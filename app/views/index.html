Find out how to meet the technology standards for IT services and digital equipment for all schools and colleges

  1. Contents


    2. These standards are to support your school or college use the right digital infrastructure and technology.

    Following these standards can help make more informed decisions about technology leading to safer, more cost-efficient practices and new learning opportunities for pupils.

    The standards are to be used by everyone involved in the planning and implementing of technology within schools and colleges, including:

    • senior leadership team
    • teachers
    • IT staff
    • suppliers
    • technical advisors

    These standards are to help your school or college with:

    • planning budget allocated to the procurement and maintenance of technology
    • buying new technology
    • investing in digital infrastructure
    • increase flexibility for learning
    • renewing a contract with a technology provider to ensure what they buy meets the needs of your school or college
    • installing new technology equipment to ensure it is properly configured or set-up

    You should:

    1. Read the standard and sub-categories.

    2. Review the standards and see if your school or college meets them.

    3. Speak with your ICT supplier or in-house support team to find out what can be done if you’re not currently meeting the standards.
